## Assignment Objective

The objective of this assignment was to identify and evaluate potential target companies that fit DeepThought's Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) using structured business research and qualification criteria.

## Deliverables Included

### 1. Target Company Research Sheet

A curated list of 25 aerospace, defence, and advanced manufacturing companies evaluated using:

* Revenue fit
* Leadership quality
* Growth signals
* Industry tailwinds
* Systems maturity
* Strategic differentiation

### 2. Methodology Document

Explains:

* Research approach
* Data sources used
* Qualification framework
* Evaluation process

### 3. 1000 Company Proposal

A scalable strategy for identifying and qualifying 1000 ICP-aligned companies using a combination of research, automation, and manual verification.

### 4. Hand-Drawn Process Diagram

Visual representation of the sourcing, filtering, scoring, and qualification workflow.
